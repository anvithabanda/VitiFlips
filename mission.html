Our Mission

Who Are We?

Hello, we are Niti and Veera—two individuals fueled by our passions and talents to create meaningful change within our community. Inspired by our experiences in India, we founded this organization with a vision to uplift underprivileged children through the transformative power of design.

Our Story

Since launching Viti.Flips, we have sold over 100 pieces of upcycled jewelry and clothing. Our creations range from re-strung necklaces and recycled bracelets to altered clothes, each piece reflecting our commitment to sustainability and creativity. Along the way, we’ve also built a thriving Instagram community with over 724 supporters, helping to spread our message and connect with like-minded individuals. In addition to the funds we've raised from our sales so far, we were able to successfuly raise $1,500 to provide winter sweaters for over 245 underprivileged children in grades 1 through 5 at the Ramasona Pay Centre School in Gujarat, India.

Our Goal

As we are continuing our journey as dedicated members of this organization we have began shifting to a new cause of spreading awarness for underprivleged children acroos the world, and building a team of young, likeminded thinkers who appriciate this project as much as we do.
